Profil Perusahaan

Since 1997, PT Aneka Coffee Industry (ACI) is proud to be endorsed by the ICO, a proponent of diversification from excessive dependence on primary-commodities via processing and industrialization: Through close collaborations with coffee organizations, ACI contributes to the significant development of coffee in the South-east Asian region, which partly shapes Indonesia's primary-commodity driven economy. Consequently, ACI adds to the value-chain by transforming raw coffee through sophisticated industrial processes, thereby generating increased consumerism and consumption. More than just coffee, we believe in nurturing a sustainable culture

Visi & Misi

PT Aneka Coffee Industry complies with the strictest international standards by utilizing modern machineries and advanced processing technologies from leading international suppliers. A dynamic and professional management team leads our company and works closely with our knowledgeable and capable staff, ranging from organoleptic connoisseurs to engineering specialists.
The foundation for PT Aneka Coffee Industry's product quality stems from stringent upstream-to-downstream quality control, beginning at the top of the supply chain with the selection and procurement of coffee beans from well-known Indonesian and international origins. Our company is strongly supported by a fully equipped, state-of-the-art analytical laboratory for full spectrum coffee analysis, including physical, chemical, microbiological, heavy metal and organoleptical parameters as required by international standards and the Indonesian National Standard (SNI).

Sejarah Perusahaan

Established in 1995, PT Aneka Coffee Industry (ACI) is the result of a collaboration between Prasidha Group's PT Prasidha Aneka Niaga Tbk-a leading coffee processor and exporter in Indonesia, Itochu Corporation-a leading Japanese trading company, and UCC Ueshima Coffee Co., Ltd.-a major player in the retail coffee industry. Located in Sidoarjo, East Java, ACI is known as one of the largest instant and roasted coffee producers in Southeast Asia with an annual production capacity of 3,600 tons of instant coffee and 2,400 tons of ground coffee.

Using the latest processing technologies from Denmark, Germany and Brazil, ACI is committed to high international standards in coffee production. Led by a professional and dynamic management team, and supported by staff who are experts in factory operations, ACI guarantees strict quality control from the selection of coffee beans to the final packing process.

Since its inception, ACI's production focus has been the international export market, with production facilities and quality control systems designed to meet high international quality standards. ACI has also obtained several important certifications such as ISO 9001:2008 and SNI 2983:2014, emphasizing its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. ACI Laboratory was even appointed as a Conformity Assessment Body in the enforcement and supervision of SNI 2983:2014 on coffee.
