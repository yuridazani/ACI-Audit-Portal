Getting Started

Follow these steps to explore the ARACI app and its features.

Navigating the ARACI App

Open the ARACI app. You can choose to start exploring the AR features through Play, learn through detailed tutorials using the Tutor, get more information about the app in the About section, or simply Exit the app when you're done.

Exploring with the AR Camera

Use pinch gestures to zoom in and out, twist to rotate the 3D models for a full 360-degree view, and explore detailed AR simulations of our coffee production process.

Using the Tutor Feature

Scan the QR code provided to download the necessary marker. Then, return to the main menu and select Play to view the 3D object using AR technology, with detailed guidance available at every step.

App Information & Exit

Learn more about the app, its development, and the team behind it by selecting About, or exit the application safely by choosing Exit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions users have about the ARACI app and its features.

To start using the ARACI app, simply download it from our website and follow the steps in the Getting Started section. Begin by selecting "Play" to explore AR features, "Tutor" for tutorials, or "About" to learn more about the app.

The ARACI app is currently available only for Android devices. It requires Android 5.0 and up. Please ensure your device has the latest updates for the best experience.

You can scan QR codes or markers directly through the ARACI app. The codes are available on our website, and by scanning them, you can unlock additional content and features within the app.

You can download the ARACI app directly from our website. Simply visit the download section, and the app will be available for download to your Android device.

Currently, there are no updates available for the ARACI app. We are working on improvements and will notify users through the website when a new update is released.

The ARACI app requires an internet connection for downloading markers and accessing updated content. However, once you have downloaded the necessary data, some parts of the app can be used offline.
