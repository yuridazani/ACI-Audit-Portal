Getting Started

Follow these steps to explore the ARACI app and its features.

Navigating the ARACI App

  • Open the app.
  • Select: Play, Tutor, About, or Exit.

Exploring with the AR Camera

  • Pinch to Zoom: Adjust the view.
  • Rotate with a Twist: View from different angles.
  • Audio Guide: Listen to commentary.
  • Info Panel: See the building overview.
  • Exit AR Camera: Return to the main menu.

Using the Tutor Feature

  • Scan the QR code: Download the marker.
  • Return to main menu: Start your session.
  • Display the 3D object: View in AR.

App Information & Exit

  • About: Learn about the app and developers.
  • Exit: Close the app.
